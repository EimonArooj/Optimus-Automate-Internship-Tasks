## Key Findings and Business Insights

* The **average total price increased from 2023 to 2024**, indicating improved sales performance, before declining in **2025**, which may suggest a reduction in sales or incomplete yearly data.

* **Printers** recorded the highest average total price among all product categories, while **Phones** generated the lowest average sales, highlighting differences in product profitability.

* The **North region** achieved the highest average total price, whereas the **South region** showed the lowest performance, indicating opportunities to strengthen sales strategies in lower-performing regions.

* Monthly analysis of **returned and non-returned orders** reveals noticeable fluctuations throughout the year, suggesting that customer return patterns vary by month and can influence overall revenue.

* The dashboard reports an **average discount of 7%**, indicating that the company maintains a moderate discount strategy while preserving product value.

* The business generated a **total sales value of approximately 4.38 million**, reflecting strong overall revenue during the analyzed period.

* Variations in **shipping costs across regions** indicate potential opportunities to optimize logistics and transportation expenses.

* Overall, the dashboard enables management to identify high-performing products and regions, monitor return trends, evaluate pricing strategies, and support data-driven decisions for improving future sales and profitability.
